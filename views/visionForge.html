Vision Forge

  1. Home
  2. Blogs

Beginning with the basics of Verilog, we designed half adders and one to two decoders, gradually advancing to JK flip flops and T flip flops. Moreover, mini projects such as designing an Alarm Clock and Car Parking System enhanced my understanding of Verilog's real- world applications.

The project also included Image Processing, where assignments on Circle detection and Image Segmentation using methods such as K means clustering, improved my skills.

All the given assignments were discussed every week, ensuring my progress and I had amazing project leaders who always helped us solve any doubts. Overall, it was an amazing learning experience.
